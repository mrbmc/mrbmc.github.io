# Privacy Policy

## Your data is yours.<br />Your activity is yours. 

- No cookies. 
- No tracking pixels.
- No login widgets.
- No bullshit.

**Analytics:** There are no analytics products slowing down your experience and selling your browsing history to the next bidder. The only metrics I have are [CDN caching reports](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/AmazonCloudFront/latest/DeveloperGuide/reports.html) that can't track sessions. 

**Third Party Code:** There are no third party libraries or code loaded that could track your visit to this site. Everything you need to view this site is hosted together on one origin point that I control.

**Permissions:** There is no local storage, cookies, or sensor access permissions needed. This site uses W3C standard and agreed upon device permissions.

**Data collection:** There is no data collection.
