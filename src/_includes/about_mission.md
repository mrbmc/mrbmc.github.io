## Useful, Usable, Unique

For 20+ years I've tried to design, build, and manage software that is useful to people – from supply-chain tools in the music industry, to the [creator-economy for podcasters](/portfolio/blogtalkradio/), and even [communal viewing for streaming](/portfolio/disney-groupwatch/). I've proven that designing to meet human needs will drive business impact.

The most powerful project you can design as leader is the team that makes products. Over the last 10 years, I've [designed high-performing teams](/portfolio/disney-growth/#project-team) that can deliver through ambiguity at any scale. Mentoring & coaching new leaders, shaping organizations, and [building culture](/portfolio/team-charter-workshop/) has been some of the most rewarding work.

Currently, I'm working with mission-driven startups to bring new products to market and level up their design literacy.
