* Paid Media
* Event Production
* Event Ticketing
## Product Innovations
### UGC
Our most used feature was our calendar of events around the New York area. Being the authoritative source required sourcing info on the ground. We leveraged relationships with venues, promoters, and the community to keep our calendar up-to-date. This means our staff acted as moderators and curators rather than authors. Shifting this responsibility enabled us to scale.

![](/images/portfolio/rhythmism/rhythmism-sketch.png)
### UGC Automation
Tapped into the technology that our clients understood - email - to streamline workflows for creating rich visual event listings and content for guests.
### Photo Sharing
In the days before instagram... We set up a realtime photo sharing feature.

"Mobilism" enabled people to send photos from their phone and post them on the web. Making this a transparent and secure experience for users required significant backend complexity. We needed to filter spam, and authenticate users, identify photo attachements, then transform them for the web.
