I've seen a few important themes over my 16 years working on "Growth Design" for Disney, MLB, BlogTalkRadio, et al. It's easy to get folx attention with a fortune cookie length maxim but you need the evidence to back it up. There is a story behind each of these.

1. "Growth" is a business goal. It's hard to be user-centered with a business-centered name.
1. ```Conversion = Motivation / Friction```
1. The biggest design challenge of Growth Design is communicating value.
1. “We Buy On Emotion and Justify with Logic.”
1. Nobody walks on the lot who doesn’t want to buy.
1. For every basis change in conversion rate there is an opposite but equal change in LTV.
1. If your payment step is leaking, the problem is in your offer step.
1. There’s no such thing as giving prospects too much information about your product.
1. Organic food, organic clothing, organic user growth.
1. Velocity is not a KPI.
