Motionbox Video Player

Motionbox Video Mixer (requires account)

 Motionbox is a video sharing site that offers people the easiest way to share and edit small clips from larger videos. The video player I developed for them has a filmstrip showing the entire video at a glance allowing instant and nonlinear access to the video. The video mixer allows people to trim and splice video clips together and save the result. The playback and mixing applications were built using Adobe's Flex framework - the latest iteration of the Flash family.
