east coast brain trust navigation

this is the global navigation device for the eastcoastbraintrust site.

Initial user testing revealed that dragging and throwing the bar around was awkward for people without a traditional mouse, such as a trackpad or one of those freaky little IBM nipples for example, so we added the scroll buttons.

We also learned that because people skim website material instead of reading, they did not notice when the scroller was repeating so we added the position indicator.

it's all about the usability testing.
