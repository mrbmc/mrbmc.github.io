#### Project Spotlight: Quality of Service

The first goal of any streaming service is the delivery of sterling quality video quickly and flawlessly. 

> "I liken it to trying to live on Mercury. The planet is completely inhospitable. Every day you're ... in a place that really does not want you. Streaming video on the internet is sort of like that." -Joe Inzerillo, Disney Streaming CTO

Half of the battle is acutally knowing in detail what kind of viewing experience our guests are having. We built a product that monitors video quality every step of the way from glass-to-glass, or camera lens to playback device, to ensure we could diagnose issues and continually improve the playback experience.
