## Design & Product Leader

I deeply believe that putting people at the center of everything you do - as a leader, as a designer, as an entrpreneur - leads to good work and good business outcomes.

Most recently I led Enterprise product design at <img src="/2023/images/disney-logo.svg" alt="Disney" style="height:1em;width:auto;display:inline;" />for our streaming services [Disney+](https://www.disneyplus.com/), [Hulu](https://www.hulu.com/), [STAR+](https://www.starplus.com/), and [ESPN+](https://espnplus.com/). Previously I led design for Growth (propelling Disney+ & ESPN+ to 130M subscribers in 2 years!), UX Research and Sports Products. Before joining Disney, I built high performing teams at startups, and enterprises using a customer-first mindset and cross-functional collaboration.

My leadership approach is based on my core values of **curiosity** and **authenticity**. I have also been told that I excel at inspirational leadership and product strategy; but the work I’m most proud of is expanding design’s business impact and mentoring design leaders.
