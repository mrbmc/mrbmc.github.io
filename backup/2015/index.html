UX Designer
Product Builder

I am a UX ronin; helping startups better serve their customers by applying design thinking and data-driven product management. I've been creating web products and apps since 1996 when I shared my first website from my NYC dorm room with friends on the other side of the planet. I was hooked by the power to connect with people around the world instantly.

In 2000 I founded a design agency to help music industry clients. As clients came in and my own ideas piled up I had to teach myself programming to keep up. When Napster and the iPod bankrupted my clients I went to work as an engineer for several startups. Then in 2008 I ran my first usability test and the participants ripped apart my wireframes. I loved the insights and pivoted back to a user-centered design practice.

The common thread through all this has been the passion to build products people love to use and the belief that great products are the fusion of empathetic design and innovative engineering.

and let's build something amazing together.

When I can be pulled away from the screen I love to travel this big amazing world, spending a lot of time underwater on the way, but I always come home to New York to cook for friends and family.

I ride my bike everyday so I can eat more bacon.
